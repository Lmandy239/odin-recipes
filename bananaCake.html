Banana Cake Recipe

The Banana cake is a delicious and moist dessert that features the natural sweetness and flavor of
ripe bananas. It is a popular choice for those who enjoy the taste of bananas in a baked treat. Banana
cakes typically have a light and tender crumb with a subtle banana aroma.





Ingredients:

  • Ripe Bananas.
  • All-purpose flour.
  • Granulated sugar.
  • Unsalted butte.r
  • Eggs.
  • Baking powde.r
  • Baking soda.
  • Salt.
  • Vanilla extract(optional).
  • Milk(optional).

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to a temperature of 350 degrees Farenheit. Grease and flour cake pan or line
    it with parchment paper for easy removal.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth using a fork or a potato masher.
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together the unsalted butter and granulated sugar until light
    and fluffy. You can use an electric mixer or beat by hand with a whisk.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time to the butter-sugar mixture, beating well after each addition. Stir
    in the vanilla extract if desired.
  5. In another bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the ingredients to the butter-sugar mixture, alternating with the mashed bananas
    Mix together until combined. Avoid overmixing, as it can result in a denser texture.
  7. If the batter seems too thick, you can add a small amount of milk to it.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan, spreading evenly.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for approximately 30-35 minutes.
  10. Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool for about 10 minutes.
  11. You may add frosting or toppings of your liking.
  12. Finally, take a seat and just enjoy your fresh Banana cake!.
